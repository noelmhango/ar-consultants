About Us

With a wealth of expertise, we are records and information management specialists

Who Are We?

AR Records and Information Management Consultants was established in 2016 and is duly registered in Malawi. The firm aims to equip staff and institutions with critical knowledge and skills in handling critical information assets.

The firm is synonymous with excellence in records and information management. Our organization is composed of a team of experts, each dedicated to advancing the practice of information management in Malawi and the wider region.

We endeavours to improve records, archives and information management functions in institutions in Malawi and beyond in the context of legislations such as Access to Information and technological advancements

Our Vision

To stand as the preeminent provider of records and information management solutions in worldwide.

We aspire to be recognized for our commitment to innovation and the pursuit of excellence in our field.

Our goal is to guide organizations in achieving their information management objectives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to equip individuals and institutions with the essential knowledge and skills required for the proficient management of information assets.

We are dedicated to improving records, archives, and information management practices within both public and private institutions, fostering adherence to legislative frameworks such as the Access to Information Act, and embracing the opportunities presented by technological advancements.

Our Team

Austine Phiri

Chief Excutive Officer

Austine Phiri is a leading consultant and expert on Information Management, Records Management, Archives Management, Digital Library Systems, Research Policy, Innovation, Science Diplomacy and Governance. He is also a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Information Sciences at Mzuzu University.

Austine holds a Master's and Bachelor's Degrees in Library and Information Science. He is enrolled at the University of South Africa finalizing his PhD in Information Sciences.

Gift Noniwa

Training and Communications Director

Gift, a seasoned Information Science professional with over a decade of experience, specializes in records and Information management and digitization. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Library and Information Science (BLIS), has excelled in both governmental and non-governmental settings, collaborating with World Food Program and World Vision Malawi.

His impactful contributions extend to research initiatives with Centre for Social Research (CSR), Centre for Educational Research and Trainings (Cert), Kamuzu University of Health Sciences(KUHEs), etc. With project management proficiency demonstrated at KUHeS, and Emmanuel International (EI), and administrative acumen at Pharmacy and Medicines Regulatory Authority (PMRA), Gift navigates the complexities of information science, research, and administration with dedication and expertise.
